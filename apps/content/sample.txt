New Page
========
You can create internal links [like this](/terms.html)  
You can create [external links](http://www.dynamic-solutions.com)

When every you want to create a new page you just create the link for the page, save your changes and then click on the new link you created.

Bullets are easy too:

* Bullet One

* Bullet Two

* Bullet Three

Or if you prefer numbered bullets, they are just as easy.

1. One numbered bullet

1. Two numbered bullets

1. Numbered bullets


Headings are done by underlining with ==== or ----.

Like this

Heading One
===========
This is the large heading

Heading Two
-----------
This is the smaller heading.
